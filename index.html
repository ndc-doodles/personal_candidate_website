About EPS

Edappadi K. Palaniswami, commonly known as EPS, is an Indian politician and former Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu. He is a prominent leader of the All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (AIADMK) party and has played a key role in the political and developmental affairs of the state.

Known for his grassroots leadership and administrative experience, EPS has focused on infrastructure development, welfare programs and initiatives to improve the lives of Tamil Nadu’s citizens throughout his political career.

“ A VISION FOR A STRONG TAMIL NADU. ”

Vision

Edappadi K. Palaniswami envisions a Tamil Nadu where progress, integrity and inclusivity go hand in hand. His vision is to shape a future built on transparency, equality and opportunity for every citizen - from vibrant urban centers to the most remote villages. He believes that true growth emerges from empowering people through education, technology and good governance rooted in honesty and service.

Driven by his deep commitment to public service, EPS focuses on strengthening rural development, enhancing agricultural support for farmers, creating opportunities for youth through skill development, and positioning Tamil Nadu as a national leader in innovation and sustainable growth. His goal is not merely political progress, but a people-centered transformation that builds unity, restores faith in governance and instills pride in the hearts of every Tamil.

Political Journey & Public Service

A Visionary Leader Guiding Tamil Nadu’s Growth

Edappadi K. Palaniswami (EPS) is widely respected for his dedicated, transparent, and development-oriented leadership. Rising from a humble background in Salem district to become the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu, he has earned a reputation as a people’s leader focused on welfare, growth, and good governance.

Under his administration, Tamil Nadu witnessed significant progress in infrastructure, agriculture, healthcare, and education. EPS championed major road and irrigation projects, empowered women through welfare schemes, promoted industrial investment, and ensured social harmony across communities. His leadership continues to emphasize unity, accountability, and inclusive development for every citizen.

Schemes & Social Initiatives

2019

Solar Rooftop Capital Incentive Scheme

Introduced by EPS to promote clean energy adoption across Tamil Nadu, providing capital subsidies to households and institutions installing solar rooftop systems. The initiative aimed to make Tamil Nadu a leader in renewable energy.

2018

Uzhavan App – Farmer Welfare Initiative

Launched to digitally empower farmers, the Uzhavan mobile app provided real-time weather updates, crop insurance details, subsidy information, and direct communication with the Agriculture Department for transparency and support.

2019

Amma Patrol – Women Safety Initiative

EPS launched the Amma Patrol vehicles to strengthen women’s safety across Tamil Nadu. The patrols, equipped with trained officers and helpline access, focused on preventing crimes against women and ensuring safer public spaces.
